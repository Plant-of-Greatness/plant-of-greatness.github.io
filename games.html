The Legend of Org

A fast-paced metriodvania where you are not necessarily the main character, that boasts with rich lore, a simulated ecosystem of an interlocking chain of robots and procedural animation for a more natural feel. All made in Godot 4 to prove a point.

You are a Cortempris Neuronrunner, a small and crafty robot that has recently been infected with a viral entity known as Orgonin. Your job is whatever you decide it to be, will you serve one of the members of the Pantheon? Will you follow your instincts, where ever it leads? Or will you descend into great depths to release an ancient evil?

Thorn: The Knight of the Fifteenth City

A small, more traditional metriodvania with fast-paced, adrenaline driven combat with a strange and enticing world and a retro style

You are simply known as Thorn, the knight and champion of the City of Thorns a hidden city, tucked away in a forgotten corner of the kingdom. Your city has waged war on the Kingdom of Narkhir. It is your job to defeat the thirteen Knights and finally King Nebloqi. Are you up for the challenge?
